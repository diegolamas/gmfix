How-To

  1. Log in using your Google account and an app password. You can generate one here.
  2. Playlist: Backup and restore your playlists.
  3. Setlist.FM to Play Music: Create playlists based on concerts found on setlist.fm webpage. You can find the setlist ID at the end of its url, it looks like 7bcc52c0. This uses the setlist.fm API.
