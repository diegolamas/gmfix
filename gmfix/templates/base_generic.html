How-To

  1. Log in using your Google account and an app password. You can generate one here.
  2. Playlist tab: You can backup to the database your playlists or you can restore the playlists on database to your Google Play Music.
  3. Setlist.FM to Play Music tab: You can create playlists based on concerts found on setlist.fm webpage. This uses the setlist.fm API.
